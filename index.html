  2. Choose Far Chase cam
  3. Activate camera setting using Ctrl-F12
  4. GROUP: Uncheck Use temporary edits
  1. Click CREATE
  2. Change group name to LL_FrontFar (Far Front)
  3. CAMERA: Click INSERT
  4. GROUP: Move previous camera back to Available list on the right
  5. CAMERA: Make these edits:
    Aim: Static X: 26 P: -17 Z: 24 POV: 78 Mic:
  1. GROUP: Click CREATE
  2. Change group name to LL_Top (Top Down)
  3. CAMERA: Click INSERT
  4. GROUP: Move previous camera back to Available list on the right
  5. CAMERA: Make these edits:
    Aim: AtCar X: -4 P: Z: 22 POV: 60 Mic:
  1. GROUP: Click CREATE
  2. Change group name to LL_Chase (Drone Chase)

Also keep in mind...

  • If the focus car changes unexpectedly during the process, and you can only select the scenic camera, hitting the live button () should allow you to once again choose other cameras and resume the process. If this still does not work, try Ctrl-F12 to close out of camera settings, and then Ctrl-F12 to resume.
  • Regarding display order, note that when CREATE is clicked, the new camera will always be listed after the currently active camera.
  • You cannot reorder cameras once they are created.
