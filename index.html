  1. Click +

  5. Make these edits:
    X: -15 Z: 3 POV: 30
    Focus Depth: 26 Aperture: 1.0
  6. Group Edit: Move previous Camera back to Available list
  7. Move group to the top of the Group list
  1. Select the "Chase" cam
