Also keep in mind...

  • If the focus car changes unexpectedly during the process, and you can only select the scenic Camera, hitting the live button should allow you to once again choose other Cameras and resume the process. If this still does not work, try to close out of the camera and open it up again.
  • Regarding display order, note that when a new Camera Group is created, it will always be listed after the currently active Camera Group.
  • You cannot reorder Camera Groups once they are created.
  • You cannot create new Cameras with the position type of "On Car". You can't even override them, they will automatically reset back to their default settings every time you load into a session.
